03 — The problem Nurami solves

Why this conversation matters to a surgeon.

The webinar only works if it speaks to a real clinical tension. Here is the one Nurami sits on top of, in the surgeon's own terms.

  • /01

    CSF leakage is a real and costly complication

    Cerebrospinal fluid leaks are a major post-neurosurgical complication, with reported rates spanning a wide range by surgical site, driving infections, longer hospital stays, and a heavy economic burden on health systems.

  • /02

    The incumbents carry trade-offs surgeons quietly dislike

    Many widely used grafts are derived from bovine or other animal tissue. That introduces sourcing constraints and a disease-transmission question, and some options still require added sealants to achieve watertight closure.

  • /03

    Watertight closure is hard exactly when it matters most

    Surgeons describe sealing the dura as genuinely difficult in the hardest cases: large defects, minimally invasive approaches, or where there is little viable tissue. The need for a thin, pliable, sutureable substitute is well documented.

  • /04

    Nurami's answer is a genuinely different material

    ArtiFascia is an FDA-cleared, fully synthetic, sutureable dural repair graft built from electrospun nanofibers, with no animal- or human-sourced materials. In its clinical study it showed a markedly lower CSF leakage rate than the control group, the exact proof point a webinar can dramatize.

  • /05

    The pipeline is a story, not just a product

    With ArtiFix, a sutureless, adhesive sealing-and-healing patch advancing through its clinical pathway, Nurami has the narrative of a platform, not a one-off. That arc is what makes two surgeons want to spend an hour in conversation.

05 — Scope of work

What we will actually do.

One webinar, run end to end. Six workstreams, each with a clear owner and a clear deliverable. This is the full operational layer wrapped around the surgeons' content.

  • /01

    Research & audience strategy

    Define the target segments, sharpen the topic and title with the two surgeons, and map the conference list into usable, compliant audiences. The strategic groundwork that makes every downstream channel land.

  • /02

    Platform setup & host enablement

    Stand up a webinar platform from scratch, since none exists today. Configure registration, reminders, and the live room, then coach your VP Commercial into a confident host, including a dry run before the live date.

  • /03

    Organic LinkedIn: 10 posts + employee amplification kit

    Ten organic posts across the campaign window, written to build anticipation and credibility, plus a ready-to-share kit so Nurami employees and the two surgeons amplify to their own networks at zero media cost.

  • /04

    Email campaign: ~10 emails, pre and post

    A full lifecycle of around ten emails: invitation and reminder sequences to the conference list before the event, and a follow-up flow after it that delivers the recording and routes engaged contacts toward a conversation. Built to work with Pipedrive.

  • /05

    Paid LinkedIn & registration management

    A focused paid LinkedIn program to extend reach to look-alike specialists and retarget warm non-registrants, plus active management of the registration list: deduping, segmenting, and keeping data clean for handoff.

  • /06

    Reporting & post-event readout

    A single source of truth tracking the funnel from reach to registration to attendance, plus a post-event readout you can take to management: what happened, what it cost, who showed up, and what to do with them next.

5,000+ → 500 engaged · ~10%

Reach is broad, then it narrows fast

Between the 1,000+ warm conference contacts worked multiple times and paid extension to look-alike specialists, total reach clears 5,000. Around 10% engage by clicking or viewing, which is a healthy rate for a clinical B2B audience.

500 → 220+ page visits · ~44%

The landing page does real work

Nearly half of engaged contacts reach the registration page. A focused, single-purpose page with the two surgeons front and center is what holds that rate.

220+ → 45 register · ~20%

Registration is a disciplined ask

About one in five page visitors registers. With a low-friction form and a credible clinical hook, 45 registrations is realistic, not a stretch. Email to the owned list is the single biggest driver.

45 → 15+ attend · ~33%

Show-up rate is where events are won

A 33%+ live attendance rate on a B2B webinar is healthy and achievable with a tight reminder sequence, calendar holds, and day-of nudges. Hitting 15 of 45 is the real target, and the reminder engine is built for it.
