03 — Boosting & paid promotion

Boost what works. Pause what doesn't.

Boosting and paid promotion are part of the scope — not now, but once organic demand is established. We will handle this the same way we run boosting for Lumenis: amplify organic content that is already proving itself, and let performance decide where the budget goes.

01

Boost organic

02

Track performance against engagement and campaign objectives across both brands and both platforms.

03

Continue investing in the strongest-performing posts, scaling spend behind what the audience is responding to.

01 — The approach

Presence first. Paid when the data says so.

Before scaling media spend, Pollogen needs a strong digital asset and a real, searchable presence. This project builds that foundation: website and landing pages, SEO, and a GEO layer that ensures the brand shows up when buyers ask AI search surfaces — not only when they type into Google. A structured plan, executed and measured, tells you where the demand is. That evidence is what makes a later paid investment a decision rather than a bet.
