01 — The setup

The strategy earned a yes. The scope deserves a real choice.

Over the past two months we mapped Pollogen's social footprint, studied the competitive set from HydraFacial to Medicube to YA-MAN, and built a strategy around the thing no competitor is doing: telling the science story. OxyGeneo™, the Bohr Effect, TriPollar RF. Real clinical depth, made visual and shareable, published with the consistency the brand has been missing.

The direction is agreed. Education-first content. Distinct roles for each brand page. A distributor enablement layer that turns every post into an asset your partners can forward. Demand that travels from the feed, through the distributor, to the clinic.

What this document settles is the footprint. Three brand pages or two. Full cadence or half. Four configurations, priced clearly, with everything they share spelled out first and a side-by-side comparison at the end. No hidden math.

02 — What never changes

The system is the system. Every option runs all of it.

Before the options, the constants. These eight commitments are identical in every configuration on this page. The choice you make changes how many pages we run and how often we publish. It never changes how we work.

  • /01

    Three channels, every page

    Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok on every brand page in scope. These three cover the full discovery journey: B2B credibility on LinkedIn, treatment decision on Instagram, trend amplification on TikTok. Profile setup and optimization for any channel that doesn't exist yet is included.

  • /02

    The science content series

    Two series per month explaining OxyGeneo™, the Bohr Effect, and TriPollar RF in visually compelling Reels and carousels. Shareable, not academic. This is the biggest untapped differentiator in the category and it ships in every option.

  • /03

    The distributor asset kit

    A co-brandable template library, refreshed monthly, that distributors can localize and deploy without a designer. Education builds the market; the kit makes sure it travels through every region's existing relationships.

  • /04

    Before and after curation

    Two to four assets per month, curated from clinical results and the in-house treatment loop, formatted per market. Visual evidence is the format every competitor needs and few do well.

  • /05

    Community management, all platforms

    24-hour response across every active page. Comments, DMs, and conversations handled in brand voice, with sensitive inquiries escalated. Proactive engagement, not just moderation. (Included in Options 01, 02, and 03. Available as an add-on for Option 04.)

  • /06

    Boosting management

    We amplify the top-performing content each month against the right audiences in Europe. The media budget of $3,000 per month sits outside the retainer and is managed transparently; the strategy, setup, and optimization are part of every option.

  • /07

    Monthly reporting and KPI review

    One consolidated report per month covering engagement, engagement rate, reach, and distributor asset adoption, reviewed together. Baselines first, targets after 60 to 90 days of consistent data.

  • /08

    A dedicated account team

    Discovery, an account brief that aligns everyone on messaging and strategy, weekly or biweekly syncs, and a direct point of contact. The same pod in every option, introduced at the end of this page.

07 — What we measure

Baselines first. Then targets we can defend.

We're not inventing targets before the data exists. The first 60 to 90 days establish the baseline; every month after that, four numbers tell us whether demand is actually traveling.

Engagement
Total interactions across all platforms: likes, comments, saves, shares. The raw signal of how content lands.
Eng. rate
Interactions as a share of reach. The industry reference point is 1.9%; we benchmark against it once baseline data exists.
Reach
Unique accounts reached per month across all channels. How far content travels beyond the existing follower base.
Adoption
The share of distributors publishing or resharing brand assets each month. The signal that the enablement layer is working.
