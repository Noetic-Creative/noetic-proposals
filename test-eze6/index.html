Introduction

Test operates in a competitive B2B financial services market where buying cycles are long and decision-makers are difficult to reach. This proposal outlines a full-funnel paid media and email strategy built to generate qualified leads at scale, targeting the companies most likely to need what Test offers. With a $50,000 monthly ad spend and coordinated activity across Paid Search, Paid Social, and Email Marketing, every channel has a defined role in moving prospects from awareness to conversion.

Discovery & Onboarding

The first 30 days are dedicated to building the strategic and creative foundation before a single dollar goes live. We run a structured kick-off to align on business objectives, target account profiles, and lead quality benchmarks. From there, we conduct audience and competitive research specific to B2B financial services, map the buyer journey, and produce a channel brief that governs messaging and targeting across all three services. Because Test does not have a brand manual, we also complete a Visual Alignment exercise to establish approved color, typography, and creative guardrails that will govern all ad creative going forward.

  • —Kick-off call to define lead goals, ICP, and success metrics
  • —Audience and competitive research across Paid Search and Paid Social landscapes
  • —Channel strategy brief covering messaging hierarchy and funnel stage mapping
  • —Visual Alignment package: color palette, typography, creative guardrails
  • —Platform account audits and tracking verification (Google Tag, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag)
  • —Conversion tracking setup and UTM framework
Paid Search

Paid Search captures demand from financial decision-makers who are already researching solutions. We build and manage Google Search campaigns targeting high-intent keywords tied to the financial services Test provides, with tightly themed ad groups, audience layering, and bid strategies optimized for lead volume at the $15,000–$50,000 spend range. Ad copy is written specifically for B2B buyers, emphasizing credibility, specificity, and a clear call to action. We continuously test headlines and descriptions, prune non-converting search terms, and adjust bids based on lead quality data fed back from the CRM or form submissions.

  • —Google Search campaign buildout with B2B-specific keyword targeting
  • —Ad copy written for financial services buyers, minimum 2 responsive search ads per ad group
  • —Negative keyword list development and ongoing refinement
  • —Audience layering using in-market, remarketing, and customer match segments
  • —Bid strategy management optimized toward lead conversion goals
  • —Monthly search term and query analysis
  • —Landing page review and conversion rate recommendations
Paid Social

Paid Social on Meta and LinkedIn runs upper and mid-funnel, reaching financial decision-makers before they are actively searching. LinkedIn is the primary channel for precise B2B targeting by job title, company size, industry, and seniority, which maps directly to Test's target buyer profile. Meta extends reach and handles retargeting at a lower cost per impression, keeping Test visible across the consideration phase. All creative is repurposed from existing Test assets and formatted for each placement. We manage campaign structure, audience builds, budget pacing, and creative rotation across both platforms.

  • —LinkedIn campaign buildout targeting by job title, seniority, industry, and company size
  • —Meta campaign buildout covering awareness and retargeting objectives
  • —Repurposed creative formatted for LinkedIn single image, carousel, and Meta feed and story placements
  • —Audience segmentation: cold, warm, and retargeting tiers on both platforms
  • —A/B testing of audience segments and creative variations
  • —Budget pacing and platform-level optimization across both channels
  • —Community management is not included in this scope
Email Marketing

Email supports lead nurturing for prospects who have entered the funnel but have not yet converted, and it re-engages contacts in Test's existing database. We build and manage campaigns within Test's email platform, writing copy and sequencing sends around the B2B sales cycle. Flows are structured to deliver relevant content at each stage of consideration, keeping Test's brand and offer present throughout a longer buying process. All email creative follows the Visual Alignment standards established during onboarding.

  • —Email platform audit and list segmentation setup
  • —Up to 4 campaign sends per month
  • —Copy and layout for each send, formatted for B2B financial services audience
  • —1 nurture sequence (up to 4 emails) targeting mid-funnel leads
  • —List hygiene and suppression management
  • —Performance reporting on open rate, click rate, and lead attribution

Reporting & Account Management

Test will have a dedicated Account Manager who owns the relationship, monitors performance daily, and flags issues before they affect results. Reporting is delivered on a consistent cadence with data tied directly to lead generation outcomes, not vanity metrics.

  • —Dedicated Account Manager
  • —Monthly performance report covering leads, CPL, ROAS, and channel-level breakdown
  • —Weekly check-in meetings
  • —Agency Analytics dashboard with real-time campaign access
