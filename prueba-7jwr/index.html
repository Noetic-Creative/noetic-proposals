02 — Scope of Work

Every deliverable, every month.

What follows is the complete list of what Noetic delivers each month as part of this engagement. Nothing is implied or assumed.

Monthly Performance Review & Campaign Adjustments

Monthly Performance Review & Campaign Adjustments

Each month, Noetic conducts a comprehensive review of campaign performance to assess what is working, what needs to change, and where the next opportunity lies. This ensures your paid social program evolves with the data rather than running on autopilot.

  • Full-funnel performance analysis across all active campaigns
  • Assessment of creative fatigue, audience saturation, and budget efficiency
  • Strategic recommendations for the month ahead
  • Adjustment of campaign structure, targeting logic, and bidding strategies as needed
  • Documentation of changes and rationale for full transparency
Daily Monitoring & Micro-Adjustments

Daily Monitoring & Micro-Adjustments

Our team monitors your paid social campaigns every single day, catching performance shifts before they become problems and capitalizing on opportunities as they emerge. This level of attention is what separates consistent results from unpredictable ones.

  • Daily review of spend pacing, cost-per-result, and delivery
  • Real-time micro-adjustments to bids, budgets, and audience settings
  • Flagging of anomalies or platform issues with same-day response
  • Ongoing quality checks across all active ad sets and campaigns
  • Proactive communication if any urgent action is taken
Weekly Optimization

Weekly Optimization

Beyond daily monitoring, Noetic performs structured weekly optimization sessions to make meaningful improvements to budget allocation, creative rotation, and targeting. This is where learnings from the prior week get applied systematically.

  • Budget reallocation based on performance trends across active ad sets
  • Creative refresh decisions based on frequency and engagement data
  • Targeting refinements including audience exclusions, expansions, and lookalike updates
  • A/B test evaluation and next-round test setup
Ad Copy

Ad Copy

Compelling copy is the entry point for every paid social ad. Noetic writes copy variants that are built around your buyer's motivations, platform-specific behavior, and the stage of the funnel each ad is targeting. Copy is developed across the active platforms in your campaign mix.

  • Research-backed copy grounded in your audience's language and intent
  • Variants written for different funnel stages including awareness, consideration, and conversion
  • Platform-native formatting and character count optimization
  • Iterative refinement based on performance data from prior cycles
  • Alignment with your brand voice and US market positioning
Static Ad Creative

Static Ad Creative

Noetic designs scroll-stopping static ad assets built for performance, not just aesthetics. Every asset is designed with the platform environment, audience psychology, and campaign objective in mind. Assets are produced for the active platforms in your campaign.

  • Concept and design aligned with campaign strategy and funnel stage
  • On-brand visuals adapted for US market sensibilities
  • Multiple format outputs as needed for platform specifications
  • Revisions incorporated based on performance data and client feedback
  • Designed for testing across audiences and placements
Video Ad Editing

Video Ad Editing

For paid social, video remains one of the highest-performing formats when executed well. Noetic handles video editing to produce platform-optimized ad assets that capture attention in the first few seconds and drive action. Assets are edited for the platforms included in your active campaign mix.

  • Editing of raw footage or existing assets into polished ad-ready video
  • Platform-specific formatting including aspect ratios and length
  • Caption and text overlay integration for sound-off viewing
  • Alignment with campaign messaging and audience targeting strategy
  • Review and revision cycle built into delivery workflow
Weekly Performance Report & Buyer Meeting

Weekly Performance Report & Buyer Meeting

Every week, your dedicated team prepares a performance report and meets with the relevant buyer-side stakeholders to walk through results and discuss direction. This keeps decision-making grounded in current data.

  • Structured weekly report covering key performance metrics
  • Dedicated meeting to review findings with the appropriate buyer contacts
  • Discussion of upcoming priorities, creative needs, and budget considerations
  • Clear documentation of decisions and action items coming out of each session
Weekly Report Review & Account Manager Client Meeting

Weekly Report Review & Account Manager Client Meeting

In parallel with the buyer meeting, your Account Manager holds a separate client-facing meeting to review the same performance data from an operational and relationship standpoint. This ensures nothing falls through the cracks and that your team always has a clear point of contact.

  • Account Manager-led review of weekly performance outputs
  • Alignment on deliverable status, timelines, and any outstanding requests
  • Proactive surfacing of questions, blockers, or opportunities
  • Running notes and follow-up documentation shared after each call
Monthly Performance Report

Monthly Performance Report

At the end of each month, Noetic compiles a comprehensive performance report and presents it to your team. This report goes beyond weekly snapshots to deliver strategic insight and clear direction for the month ahead.

  • Full-month analysis of campaign performance across all active channels
  • Creative performance breakdown including top and underperforming assets
  • Budget efficiency review and spend-to-result summary
  • Strategic recommendations and proposed focus areas for the next month
  • Presented live with your team in a dedicated review session
