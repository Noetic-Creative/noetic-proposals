Tier 1 — Foundation
Introduction

This proposal outlines Noetic Creative's recommended approach for prueba's paid social program across Meta and TikTok. We're building the foundation layer: structured account architecture, tested creative, and a repurposed content strategy that stretches existing assets without sacrificing performance. The goal is a scalable, data-informed paid social engine that drives measurable growth from month one.

Discovery & Onboarding

The first 30 days are about getting the infrastructure right before a single dollar goes to media. We conduct a full audit of prueba's existing digital presence, competitive landscape, and audience signals. Because prueba has an existing brand manual, we move directly into channel strategy and creative briefing rather than spending time on visual alignment. By the end of week four, campaigns are built, reviewed, and ready to launch.

  • —Kick-off call to align on business objectives, KPIs, and primary audience targets
  • —Competitor and category audit across Meta and TikTok
  • —Audience segmentation and persona mapping based on platform data and brand positioning
  • —Campaign architecture build-out: naming conventions, pixel/tracking validation, account structure
  • —Creative brief development based on brand manual, positioning, and platform best practices
  • —Internal QA and client review before any campaign goes live
Paid Social — Meta & TikTok

Noetic manages prueba's full paid social presence across Meta (Facebook and Instagram) and TikTok. Both platforms are built with distinct creative strategies that reflect their respective formats and user behaviors, while keeping brand consistency intact. Creative is repurposed from existing prueba assets and adapted to meet platform specs and performance standards. We handle campaign strategy, audience builds, budget allocation, creative trafficking, and ongoing optimization. Every decision is tied to performance data, not assumptions.

  • —Full campaign management across Meta (Facebook + Instagram) and TikTok
  • —Audience research, segmentation, and targeting strategy for each platform
  • —Repurposed creative adaptation: resizing, reformatting, and copy tailoring to platform specs
  • —Ad copy writing for all active ad units
  • —Campaign structure covering prospecting and retargeting funnels
  • —Ongoing bid strategy and budget optimization
  • —A/B testing of creative variables, audiences, and placements
  • —Weekly performance monitoring with mid-cycle optimizations
  • —Monthly account performance review and strategy recommendations
UGC — User Generated Content (Repurposed)

Noetic sources, selects, and deploys repurposed UGC as paid creative for prueba's campaigns. Rather than commissioning net-new content, we work within the repurposed model: identifying high-performing organic content and existing creator assets, obtaining usage rights where needed, and editing for paid placement. This keeps creative output consistent and cost-efficient while giving campaigns the authenticity that platform algorithms and audiences respond to.

  • —Identification and selection of repurposable UGC assets aligned with campaign objectives
  • —Rights clearance coordination for selected content
  • —Creative editing and formatting for Meta and TikTok ad placements
  • —Integration of UGC into active campaign rotation alongside other creative formats
  • —Performance tracking per creative unit to identify top-performing assets
  • —Ongoing refresh recommendations based on creative fatigue signals

Paid Social — Meta & TikTok

We manage Prueba's paid social presence across Meta (Facebook and Instagram) and TikTok, operating each platform according to its own creative and algorithmic logic rather than running the same campaigns everywhere. On Meta, we build and manage full-funnel campaign structures covering prospecting and retargeting, with audience segmentation informed by pixel data and first-party signals. On TikTok, we focus on native-feeling creative formats that align with platform behavior, using the UGC content produced under this scope as primary ad material. Campaign management includes ongoing bid and budget optimization, A/B testing of creative and copy, and audience iteration based on weekly performance data.

  • —Audience build-out including interest-based, lookalike, and retargeting segments across both platforms
  • —Ad creative repurposing: reformatting and adapting existing brand assets and UGC for platform-specific specs and placements
  • —Ongoing campaign optimization: bids, budgets, audiences, and placements adjusted based on performance data
  • —A/B testing framework for creative, copy, and audience variables
  • —Pixel and conversion event monitoring to maintain tracking accuracy
  • —Platform-specific reporting with channel-level spend and performance breakdown
