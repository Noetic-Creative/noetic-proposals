01 — Introduction

Presence that earns attention.

Rail Europe has spent decades positioning itself as the premier gateway for travelers booking European rail journeys from North America and beyond, offering access to hundreds of train operators, passes, and point-to-point tickets through a single, trusted platform. The Swiss Bliss 2026 campaign represents a meaningful departure from the transactional nature of most rail booking experiences. Rather than leading with schedules and prices, Rail Europe and Swiss Travel System are inviting travelers into a slower, more personal kind of storytelling, one built around curated itineraries, handpicked contributors, and a custom illustration style that evokes the feeling of receiving a postcard from someone who truly knows a place. The creative ambition here is real, and the opportunity is to build digital real estate that matches it.

The scope Noetic is proposing is built specifically around that ambition. The five-page landing page system, one main hub and four itinerary-specific pages, will be designed to function as an editorial content experience rather than a campaign microsite. Each page will be crafted to honor the custom illustration language and postcard aesthetic already developed, with on-page SEO baked into the structure from the start so the content can be discovered organically as the campaign builds momentum. Mobile and desktop versions are included, and every file will be handed off in developer-ready format so your team can move straight into build without any translation gaps. The illustration project, covering the physical postcard and the suite of individual digital elements, is scoped separately and will be developed in close coordination with the landing page system to ensure full visual cohesion across channels.

Noetic approaches this engagement as a creative partner with a stake in the outcome, not a vendor delivering files. With a target delivery in the first week of July, we will move quickly without sacrificing the craft the Swiss Bliss creative direction demands. Weekly calls will keep alignment tight and decisions moving, and you will have a dedicated point of contact throughout. This is the kind of campaign that deserves design work that can carry the weight of the story Rail Europe and Swiss Travel System are telling together.

02 — Scope of Work

Every deliverable, on time.

What follows is the complete list of what Noetic delivers as part of this engagement. Nothing is implied or assumed.

Landing Page System Design

Landing Page System Design

Noetic will design a five-page digital experience for the Swiss Bliss 2026 campaign, built to feel like a natural extension of the campaign's editorial and illustrative identity rather than a standard tourism product page. Every design decision, from typography hierarchy to image framing to scroll behavior, will serve the slow-travel, postcard-inspired creative direction Rail Europe has established.

  • 1 main campaign landing page designed for desktop and mobile
  • 4 supporting itinerary pages, one per curated Swiss rail route, designed for desktop and mobile
  • On-page SEO integrated into page structure, headings, and metadata recommendations throughout all 5 pages
  • Design system aligned to the custom illustration style and postcard aesthetic from the Swiss Bliss creative brief
  • Developer-ready handoff package including all assets, specs, and annotated files
  • 2 rounds of revisions incorporated into the design process before final handoff
Swiss Bliss Illustration Suite

Swiss Bliss Illustration Suite

Separate from the landing page system, Noetic will oversee and deliver the illustration assets required for both the physical postcard and the broader digital campaign, working in close coordination with the illustrator and the Swiss Travel System creative brief already in development.

  • 1 final postcard design, front and back, at standard A6 dimensions (148 x 105 mm), including the "This is Swiss Bliss" text treatment and Rail Europe and Switzerland Tourism logo placement
  • Editable source files for the postcard front (illustration) and back (message and address layout)
  • Individual illustrated elements extracted and optimized for digital use across landing pages, social, and other campaign touchpoints
  • All files delivered in formats suitable for both print production and web and digital content use
