The objective

Drive in-store appointment bookings.

The single conversion that matters is a booked appointment (Calendly scheduled) at one of SleePare's nine US showrooms. The target is a cost-per-appointment at or below $100. These are two brand-new channels with no pixel history, so the plan starts deliberately high and steps down toward target as the algorithms learn, with Meta reaching ~$105 by the sixth month of active delivery.

Where the numbers come from

Anchored to real Google Ads data.

There is no Meta or Reddit history to model from, so we anchor the appointment CPA to the warmest signal SleePare owns: Google Search, where users arrive with declared intent. That data sets the floor; cold social channels start above it and converge down.

Read: Google's appointment CPA averages ~$134 with a range of $109 to $180, and that is the warmest traffic SleePare has, with a mature pixel and months of optimization. A cold channel with no pixel cannot open below this, which is why Meta is modeled to start near $205 and Reddit higher still, then converge as conversion signal accumulates.

Channel 1 · 75% of budget · ~$11,250/mo

Meta, the appointment engine.

Meta carries the conversion goal because it is the only channel here with a strong appointment-optimizing engine. We run three stages. The funnel stage is defined by the audience we target, not by the campaign objective: both MOFU and BOFU optimize for Book Appointment, but they reach different temperatures of audience.

TOFUAwareness
Objective: Video Views / ThruPlay

Warms a cold audience with new video creative edited in-house specifically for Meta. This stage is not judged on CPA; its purpose is to warm the audience so we can retarget those video viewers further down the funnel.

Objective: Sales → Book Appointment · cold audience

BOFURetargeting
Objective: Sales → Book Appointment · warm audience

Same objective, hot audiences: site visitors (180d) and TOFU video viewers (booking-page drop-off retargeting to be confirmed, pending tracking setup). The lowest CPA in the plan. Budget starts small (no one to retarget at first) and grows as TOFU and MOFU fill the pools.

Channel 2 · 25% of budget · ~$3,750/mo

Reddit, the consideration feeder.

Reddit's conversion optimizer is weaker than Meta's, and the platform naturally fits the consideration stage more than the conversion stage. That is exactly why we use it here: consideration-style campaigns on Reddit complement Meta's TOFU and BOFU work, giving us a full-funnel, cross-channel strategy. Reddit also feeds warm audiences into Meta's retargeting.

1–2Traffic
Objective: Traffic → showroom landing pages

Conversation-format ads (native posts, not banners) in high-intent communities: r/Mattress, r/SleepApnea, r/BuyItForLife, r/Sleep. Judged on cost-per-click and landing-page views, not appointment CPA, since there is no pixel signal yet to optimize on. Feeds Meta's retargeting pixel and Reddit's own pixel.

Once the Reddit pixel has accumulated signal, a portion shifts to optimize for appointments. Reddit's direct CPA stays above Meta's throughout, which is expected given its role as a consideration channel rather than a primary conversion driver.

How we judge Reddit: not on its direct appointment CPA, but on cost of generating warm audience and assisted conversions. The appointment is closed by Meta's BOFU; Reddit and Meta's TOFU make that low BOFU CPA possible.

Targeting

Geo-fenced to active showrooms.

Because the conversion is an in-person appointment, both channels are hard geo-fenced to a 25 to 40 km radius around each active showroom, not entire states. Seattle is excluded until it opens (currently "coming soon").

Data-integrity note.

Anchored in data The appointment CPA curve is built from SleePare's own Google Ads Book-Appointment cost (Jul 2025 to May 2026). Cold-channel start points are set above Google's range and converge as pixel signal accumulates.

Industry benchmark CPM, CPC, CTR and frequency for Meta and Reddit are market benchmarks for US retail/home verticals; SleePare has no prior Meta or Reddit history. These could be replaced with first-party data after the first 30 to 45 days of live delivery, and the model re-baselined accordingly.

Before we spend a dollar

Pre-launch requirements.

The model assumes clean conversion tracking from day one. Neither pixel exists yet, so this is the gating item for the timeline above.

Meta Pixel + CAPI

Install on site, fire Book Appointment on Calendly schedule confirmation. Server-side CAPI for accuracy.

Reddit Pixel

Install on site, map the same appointment event so Reddit can optimize from ad month 3.

Creative

New in-house video edits for Meta TOFU, comparison and offer statics for MOFU/BOFU, native conversation posts for Reddit.
