01 — Introduction

Presence that earns attention.

As Rail Europe continues to grow its content and campaign presence, the opportunity to deepen that storytelling through editorial-quality digital experiences is significant, particularly when launching campaign-specific initiatives that need to feel distinct from the core booking product. Swiss Bliss 2026, developed in partnership with Swiss Travel System, is exactly that kind of moment: a campaign with a defined creative language, a tight launch window, and a need for landing pages that carry the weight of the editorial vision behind them.

The scope we are proposing addresses the core design challenge directly: translating a custom illustration style rooted in postcards, slow travel, and local storytelling into five developer-ready pages that feel cohesive, immersive, and distinct from a standard tourism product page. The work covers one main landing page and four supporting itinerary pages, designed for both mobile and desktop, with on-page SEO baked in from the start. Rather than retrofitting SEO considerations after the design is locked, we build those foundations into the page architecture during the design phase so the handoff to development is clean and the pages are positioned to perform from day one.

We see this project as an extension of the work we are already doing together, and our goal is to make this process as frictionless as possible given the July delivery target. You will have a dedicated point of contact throughout, a clear revision process, and designs that are ready for development without ambiguity. This proposal outlines exactly what is included, what is not, and how we will get there on time.

02 — Scope of Work

Every deliverable, on time.

What follows is the complete list of what Noetic delivers as part of this engagement. Nothing is implied or assumed.

Swiss Bliss 2026 Landing Pages

Swiss Bliss 2026 Landing Pages

Noetic will design a five-page landing page website for the Swiss Bliss 2026 campaign, built around the established illustration and editorial creative direction provided by Rail Europe and Swiss Travel System. Each page will be designed for both desktop and mobile, with on-page SEO structure included, and the full set will be packaged for a clean developer-ready handoff.

  • 1 main campaign landing page designed to orient visitors, introduce the Swiss Bliss 2026 concept, and guide them into the four itinerary experiences
  • 4 supporting itinerary pages, each paired to one of the personalized Swiss rail routes and designed to reflect the editorial and illustrative identity of the campaign
  • Desktop and mobile versions delivered for all 5 pages
  • Developer-ready handoff including organized design files, component notes, and specifications sufficient for a development team to build without requiring additional design clarification
  • 2 rounds of revisions included across the design deliverables
