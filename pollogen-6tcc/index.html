02 — The content mix

Not static posts. A video-led editorial rhythm.

2–3 posts per week per brand, across both channels combined — in a deliberately mixed format. A typical week could be one Reel, one carousel, and one static, with Stories running alongside as content and community management.

Reels

Short-form video, typically 30 to 90 seconds. The core of reach on both Instagram and TikTok.

Carousels

Multi-frame storytelling for science explainers, before and afters, and step-by-step treatment narratives.

Statics

Single-frame posts for announcements, quotes, and clean brand moments.

Stories

Community management and content extension: resharing tagged stories, pushing new feed content, announcements.

~80% Original, made per channel
~20% Repurposed

Original and tailored, not mirrored. Roughly 80% of content is created original for each channel. The remaining 20% is repurposed where it makes sense, leveraging efficiencies without flattening the brand into one identical post everywhere.

01 — The approach

Presence first. Paid when the data says so.

Before scaling media spend, Pollogen needs a strong digital asset and a real, searchable presence. A structured plan, executed and measured, tells you where the demand actually is. That evidence is what makes a later paid investment a decision rather than a bet.

This project starts at the foundation: website and landing page presence, SEO setup and ongoing activity, and a GEO layer that ensures the brand shows up when buyers ask AI search surfaces — not only when they type into Google.

Scoped and priced completely separately from social media so each can be reviewed independently on budget and merit.

04 — Technical setup & measurement

If it isn't measured, it cannot be scaled.

The whole point of starting here is to gather the evidence that informs later investment. That requires measurement infrastructure in place from day one.

  • Analytics setup and configuration across the website and landing pages
  • UTM framework and tagging discipline for every campaign and channel
  • Conversion tracking and event setup tied to real business actions
  • Search Console and indexing health monitoring
  • Structured data and schema for both SEO and GEO eligibility
  • Monthly reporting against agreed KPIs, with quarterly strategic reviews
  • A clean baseline so demand can be read accurately before any paid scale-up
