02 — Scope of Work

Every deliverable, every month.

What follows is the complete list of what Noetic delivers each month as part of this engagement. Nothing is implied or assumed. Every line below is included.

Strategy

Monthly Strategy Review

Noetic conducts a monthly review of overall performance and uses those findings to refine the content and channel strategy going forward. This ensures the program evolves with the market and never runs on autopilot.

  • Monthly performance review covering key metrics across all active channels
  • Strategic adjustments to content mix, posting frequency, and engagement approach based on data
  • Alignment of organic efforts with broader business and marketing objectives
  • Identification of emerging opportunities based on platform trends and audience behavior
Research

Social Media Goals, Trends & Competitor Research

Before content is created each month, we conduct a research pass to understand what is working in your category and what your competitors are doing. This keeps the content informed, differentiated, and strategically positioned.

  • Monthly audit of competitor social activity across relevant platforms
  • Trend analysis specific to your industry and target audience in the US market
  • Identification of content gaps and opportunities to own a unique position
  • Findings fed directly into the content calendar and creative direction for the month
Brand Voice

Content Pillars & Tone of Voice

We define and maintain a clear content framework that gives every post a strategic reason to exist. We revisit and refine the pillars as the brand evolves and the audience responds.

  • Defined content pillars that reflect the brand values and audience interests
  • Tone of voice guidelines that translate across formats and platforms
  • Guardrails that ensure consistency across educational, promotional, and community-driven posts
  • A living framework that informs all creative decisions throughout the engagement
Planning

Monthly Content Calendar Creation & QA

Each month, Noetic builds a complete content calendar that maps out every post, format, and publishing date in advance. Before anything moves into production, the calendar goes through an internal quality review.

  • Full monthly calendar with post topics, formats, and platform assignments
  • Content mapped to pillars and strategic objectives established in planning
  • Balanced mix of static and video content across the month
  • Internal review for strategic fit and brand alignment before client delivery
  • Structured approval process with clear feedback windows and version control
Creative

Static Post Design & Caption Copywriting

Noetic designs static social posts built to perform on feed — each piece crafted to stop the scroll and communicate clearly. Every post ships with a caption written to match the visual, serve the platform's algorithm, and speak to a US audience.

  • Original design for each static post, created in-house by Noetic's design team
  • Brand-consistent visuals with platform-optimized sizing and formatting
  • Original caption copy with platform-appropriate voice, calls to action, and hashtag research
  • Up to three rounds of internal review before client delivery
Video

Video Editing & Scripts

Noetic edits short-form video content for social and writes tight scripts that give each video structure and purpose. Source footage is provided by the client or sourced as agreed.

  • Professional editing of up to one minute of finished video per deliverable
  • Pacing, text overlays, transitions, and audio treatment aligned to platform norms
  • Full script for each video — hook, body, and close structured to maximize watch time
  • Scripts submitted for review and approval before production begins
Publishing

Post Scheduling & Publishing

Noetic handles all scheduling and publishing in biweekly batches, so content goes live at the right time without requiring client coordination for each individual post.

  • Scheduling of all approved content across active platforms
  • Optimal posting times based on platform data and audience behavior
  • Published in two batches per month for consistent cadence
  • Confirmation provided after each publishing batch
Reporting

Biweekly Review & Monthly Performance Report

Midway through each month and at the close, Noetic reviews performance data and delivers a comprehensive report that tells the story of what happened, what it means, and what comes next.

  • Review of reach, engagement, follower growth, and content performance
  • Identification of top-performing content and underperforming areas
  • Full monthly report with data, strategic commentary, and recommendations
  • Findings surfaced in client reporting and used to inform the month ahead
Community

Daily Community Management & Proactive Engagement

Noetic monitors and manages your social presence every day across two platforms. Beyond responding, we actively engage in relevant conversations, with relevant accounts, and within relevant communities.

  • Daily monitoring of comments, DMs, and mentions across two platforms
  • Timely, on-brand responses and escalation of sensitive inquiries
  • Outbound engagement with target accounts, partners, and industry conversations
  • Sentiment tracking and monthly community management report
