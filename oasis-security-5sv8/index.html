Analysis

Key takeaways.

What the data confirms, and what to carry forward into the next campaign phase.

✅ Audience quality is strong

85% of impressions hit 10,001+ employee organizations. IT and Engineering represent 95% of video completions. This is the right audience for an enterprise NHIM motion.

📍 Right markets activated

NYC, DFW, SF, Seattle, and DC represent the top 5 geos — all primary US enterprise security markets. Seattle's 49.4% VR and 2.41% completion rate is the strongest engagement signal geographically.

🎬 Video held through completion

~200 completions from ~300 75%+ viewers (~67% retention) on a cold audience is solid. The content was relevant enough to hold attention once someone crossed the halfway threshold.

⚡ Cisco + Hyperscaler engagement

Cisco's 4.4% completion rate and strong view rate stands out. AWS, Google, Microsoft, and Meta were all visible in top company delivery — relevant for both NHIM and AAM positioning.

🎯 Low frequency = clean slate

Low frequency is expected and appropriate for a 7-day event push. This audience hasn't been over-exposed — the segment can be reactivated for NHIM or demo campaigns without creative fatigue risk.

📊 Social actions dominated clicks

78 of ~100 clicks were social actions. For event promotion, this is expected — posts tend to generate more social engagement than direct LP traffic. The 10 LP clicks represent genuine intent signals worth noting.
