01 — Introduction

Presence that earns attention.

Tia Coffee has built something genuinely distinctive: a specialty coffee brand rooted in Israeli culture and designed for the American market, with a clean visual identity, thoughtful packaging, and a product line that spans whole beans, ground coffee, and ready-to-brew formats. The challenge Tia faces now is one of focus. After a period of brand-building investment that produced a strong foundation, the priority shifts to generating measurable, compounding returns from the assets already in place. The most immediate opportunity is capturing and converting the audience Tia has already attracted, deepening the relationship with existing customers, and building organic visibility so that new customers can find Tia without paid media spend driving every acquisition.

Email marketing and SEO are the right tools for exactly this stage. A well-structured email program turns one-time buyers into repeat customers and keeps Tia top of mind between purchases, which matters especially for a consumable product where purchase frequency is the primary lever for revenue growth. SEO compounds over time: optimizing existing pages and building content around the searches that Tia's ideal customers are already making means that every month of work builds on the last, creating a channel that does not reset when a campaign budget runs out. Together, these two services address retention and acquisition simultaneously, without requiring ad spend.

Noetic Creative has been part of Tia's story from early on, through the website, brand messaging, packaging, and email work we have already delivered together. This next engagement is a continuation of that partnership, now sharpened around the channels that will produce the most durable growth at this stage of the business. We are not starting from scratch. We know the brand, we know the voice, and we are ready to put that knowledge to work in a focused, consistent program that builds real commercial momentum for Tia.

02 — Scope of Work

Consistent. Every month

What follows is the complete list of what Noetic delivers each month as part of this engagement. Nothing is implied or assumed.

Email Marketing

Email Marketing

Noetic will run a structured email program designed to drive repeat purchases and deepen customer loyalty, leveraging the brand voice and audience understanding we have already developed with Tia. For a consumable product like specialty coffee, email is the highest-ROI retention channel available, and a consistent cadence of well-crafted campaigns keeps Tia present and relevant between purchase cycles.

  • 2 campaign per quarter, with up to 4 emails each, totaling a max of 8 emails per quarter
  • Campaign themes will rotate across product education, seasonal or cultural moments, promotions, and brand storytelling to keep engagement high and avoid fatigue
  • 1 automation flow built or refined per month, covering high-priority sequences such as welcome, post-purchase, win-back, or browse abandonment
  • Each email includes copywriting, layout direction, and subject line and preview text written for deliverability and open rate
  • List segmentation recommendations to ensure the right messages reach the right subscribers based on purchase behavior and engagement history
  • Monthly review of send performance, including open rate, click rate, and revenue attribution, to inform the following month's strategy
SEO

SEO

Noetic will build Tia's organic search presence through a combination of on-page optimization and content creation, ensuring that people searching for specialty coffee, Israeli coffee, or related terms can find and choose Tia. Because we built the site, we can move efficiently and avoid the ramp-up time that typically slows down SEO engagements.

  • Technical SEO audit completed at kickoff to identify and prioritize site-level issues affecting crawlability, indexing, and page speed
  • 1 page optimized per month, targeting commercially relevant keywords aligned with Tia's product pages, category pages, or key landing pages
  • On-page optimization for each targeted page includes title tags, meta descriptions, header structure, internal linking, and content improvements
  • Keyword research maintained on an ongoing basis to identify new opportunities as Tia's product line or market positioning evolves
  • Monthly reporting on keyword ranking movements, organic traffic trends, and content performance to track compounding progress
