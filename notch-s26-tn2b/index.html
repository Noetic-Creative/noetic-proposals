Paid Media
LinkedIn Findings

Four decisions the data made for us.

📄 Document Ads outperformed everything

Document ads drove the majority of lead activity. "See Where Insurance AI Works" hit 11% engagement rate. Static creatives drove zero leads.

🎯 First-party targeting wins on efficiency

First-party audience (1PT-US) consistently outperformed ABM company list targeting on both engagement rate and lead quality signals.

🌐 Website conversion path didn't convert

Campaigns optimized for website conversions generated near-zero leads and were deprioritized mid-flight. Root cause: no dedicated campaign landing pages.

💡 Provocative angles unlock engagement

"Most insurance AI projects focus on the wrong problems" and "Why Insurance AI Pilots Fail" showed 4–6% engagement vs. generic messaging. Challenge-based framing cuts through with C-suite and innovation leads.

Channel Mix

Two engines. One goal. Qualified leads.

Engagement layer

Lightweight awareness campaign running with document ads or carousels to build audience signal and teach the algorithm which users engage most.
