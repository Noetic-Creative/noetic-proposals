01 — What we discussed

Two conversations that covered a lot of
honest ground.

  • /01

    The checkout is where the money disappears

    FlexFactor intercepts false payment declines at the last mile — the exact moment a transaction should have cleared. $145B disappears this way every year. 55% of affected shoppers leave immediately, and 40% never come back to that brand. The problem is measurable, documented, and yours to solve.

  • /02

    A tiered account list, built from scratch

    Brennen has already done the heavy lifting: 15,000 Braintree contacts in ZoomInfo, filtered down to 2,240 US-qualified merchants, AI-enriched across transaction type and revenue band, sorted into six tiers. The raw intelligence is ready. What's missing is the marketing layer that makes those accounts aware of FlexFactor before the sales call lands.

  • /03

    Messaging needs to speak to three different kinds of pain

    Not every person on the list cares about the same thing. Revenue recovery speaks to CFOs and CROs. Wasted customer acquisition spend speaks to CMOs and VPs of Marketing. Brand loyalty and LTV erosion speaks to brand-focused leaders. The same FlexFactor story, told three different ways, to the right three people per account — that's what ABM makes possible.

  • /04

    Webinars work best when you borrow the audience

    Brennen had already started building relationships with the Merchant Risk Council (200 payment professionals) and was aligned with Braintree on a joint webinar series. The key insight from our conversation: FlexFactor doesn't need to build a webinar audience — they need to appear inside the audiences that already trust their integration partners. Production, content, and structure are where Noetic comes in.

    The Braintree motion needs to replicate

    Braintree is the proof of concept. The larger opportunity is a GTM machine that runs the same play across PayPal (who recently gave Brennen full marketing control), Worldpay, Aurus, Nuve, and eventually Stripe. Every asset we build for Braintree is infrastructure for the next ten partners. That framing shapes how we scope and prioritize the work from day one.

  • /06

    The bottleneck is time, not strategy

    Brennen and Zeev have the vision, the industry depth, and a clear picture of where FlexFactor needs to go. What they asked for wasn't another agency — it was an execution partner that can operate independently, bring ideas to the table, and make the sales team's outreach land harder. That's the role Noetic is here to play.

Email Lifecycle

Your email infrastructure is healthy. Now let's use it.

Natalia audited FlexFactor's email infrastructure as part of our preparation. Domain health, DMARC configuration, and sender reputation are all confirmed strong. What's missing is the workflow layer — segmented sequences that fire based on intent signals from ZoomInfo, LinkedIn engagement, and CRM activity.
